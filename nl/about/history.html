Log in met Minecraft


Om in te loggen op de website, start je Minecraft met de nieuwste versie van LabyMod en klikt u op uw hoofd in het Minecraft-hoofdmenu of in de LabyMod-instellingen.




Als je problemen hebt met het opstarten van Minecraft met LabyMod of je het hoofd in je hoofdmenu niet kan vinden zorg ervoor dat je dan de laatste versie van LabyMod gebruikt.

Houd er alsjeblieft rekening mee dat je Minecraft Java Edition gekocht moet hebben om te registreren.


De geschiedenis van LabyMod

2015

16.01.2015

First lines of code

LabyStudio created the first version of LabyMod because there were things he didn’t like in Vanilla Minecraft. He wanted to improve and add features which were missing.

13.04.2015

LabyMod 1.0 Release

Just three months after the first lines of code were written, LabyStudio released the first version of LabyMod, including features like a FPS, coordinates and IP display.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lCwDWLa8qBs

05.05.2015

Release of LabyMod Capes

Besides OptiFine and Mojang capes, LabyMod released its own capes. At the beginning you could use only certain cape designs.
Side fact: The first users that owned a cape were “LetsBomber”, “Sythey_” and “Proxxi”.

04.08.2015

LabyMod 2.0 Release

About four months after the release of LabyMod 1.0, an improved version with new features like the LabyChat, FriendTags, an AFK Timer, even a new website and a logo showing a wolf were created.

2016

27.05.2016

LabyMod Wings entered the Shop

After previously only being accessible to a select group of people, the famous LabyWings were available for everyone from then on and allowed users to combine their skins and the cosmetic for a new and original look!

28.10.2016

Website relaunch and new logo

Just 14 months after the last logo and website were released LabyMod published a new and modern looking design for either the website and the logo still showing a wolf. Those designs are still the ones in use. The website constantly updates, but it kept its basic style over the time.

24.12.2016

LabyMod Discord Release

We released our official Discord server on Christmas Eve of 2016, you could say as a gift to our community. The server grows steadily and is a forum for tens of thousands of LabyMod fans today, with giveaways, community interactions and direct LabyMod support.

2017

04.03.2017

1,000,000 Users

After less than 2 years, LabyMod reached the huge milestone of 1,000,000 unique users. All staff members worked together to create a short film celebrating the occasion! The video also included secret teasers for LabyMod 3.0, were you able to find them?

29.03.2017

LabyMod Merchandise Release

Merchandise was one of the most common requests by fans, so it was no surprise when our first line of merchandise was quickly sold out! We didn't just restock our shirts and hoodies, but also introduced other items over the next years, like the LabyMod cap, the LabyMod LED lamp and the LabyMod advent calendar.

20.08.2017

LabyMod 3.0 Release

LabyMod 3.0 was released to beta testers during Gamescom 2017. People who were lucky enough to catch a beta key lanyard could test the new and improved LabyMod before anyone else. And when LabyMod 3.0 eventually released publically, the servers could barely handle the hype!

2018

11.06.2018

The LabyMod World Cup Viewing

With the football World Cup in 2018, LabyMod added an addon with which you could watch the football matches in Minecraft on a huge screen. Many big German servers cooperated with us to build those screens. Lots of players had fun watching the world cup with LabyMod. We also added “Germany Wings”, which were special because you weren’t able to use two colors for the normal LabyWings. We repeated the public viewing event in other football cups, as well.

29.06.2018

2,000,000 Users

Three years after the release of LabyMod, we reached the stunning number of two million registered users, which was celebrated with a livestream event, during which we gave away merchandise as well as every cosmetic for a lifetime.

02.11.2018

VoiceChat & Emotes

This was one of the biggest updates after the release of LabyMod 3.0 as it didn’t only allow people to talk to each other in groups, but also to play funny emotes like the famous “Infinity Sit”! Funfact: Did you know that the first emotes were created by regular staff members with the use of specifically created tools?

2019

21.03.2019

New wings & every cosmetic lifetime

The LabyMod shop received a massive overhaul: not only were many new cosmetics like the Crystal Wings and Steampunk Wings introduced, but users were also able to buy all cosmetics lifetime for the very first time.

27.06.2019

First server partner

Finally, at the end of June 2019, the first important server partnerships were established and LabyMod users on Timolia and RewisServer now have special features they can use!

17.08.2019

LabyMod+

Like with the LabyMod 3.0 beta, we gave away keys for LabyMod+ during Gamescom, so users could get the new product, which provides plenty of ways to upgrade one's appearance ingame and offers certain benefits .
Along with the release of LabyMod+, the Discord was revised, offering a channel especially for LabyMod+ users.

27.10.2019

Sticker release

Cool icons and emojis? We also love them so we added our stickers to LabyMod. We released the stickers on our Halloween Event 2019. Many events got their own stickers. By the time we also added sticker packs for the communities of our partners.

2020

07.03.2020

LabyCup

LabyMod hosted a huge UHC tournament for streamers and youtubers from the german community. The winners NoRisk and NQRMAN got 1000€. It was a special UHC with flying loot drops, a cool computer voice that announced events in the game and you could hear the other teams ingame with the LabyMod VoiceChat!

17.03.2020

3,000,000 Users

It has been over 5 years since LabyStudio wrote the first lines of code and in this time, lots of different features and cosmetics were introduced. In the middle of March 2020, we celebrated the milestone of 3 million users!

13.04.2020

LabyMod 5th Birthday & first international application phase

Even though LabyMod is already 5 years old, we won't stop expanding! Together with the anniversary, members of our international community were able to apply as staff members for the first time.

03.10.2020

Laby Signature Collection

Many people loved our first merchandise collection. So we released our new Signature Collection containing a new shirt with a simple “LABY” signature. It was the first opportunity to get the Merch Crown again after a long period of time. Many people wanted a “LABY” shirt. The first shirts were sold out after 15 minutes!

2021

02.04.2021

LabyMod 1.16.5 & Classic PvP

LabyMod became compatible with the Minecraft version 1.16.5 in the update 3.8. In addition, the Classic PvP Mode was added, which allows playing with the previous PvP system on higher versions!

08.05.2021

4.000.000 Users

Soon after the release of LabyMod 1.16.5, the milestone of 4.000.000 registered users was reached.
To celebrate this, everyone was invited to claim a free partyhat cosmetic and take part in a giveaway for a lifetime cosmetic.

2022

11.03.2022

Smartphone App

With the release of our LabyMod mobile app for the Play Store and the App Store, we gave our users the opportunity to manage their Cosmetics & Emotes in an easy and comfortable way. Users can even use our shop in the app using LabyCoins. As a reward for using the app they can get a new exclusive Phone cosmetic!

28.08.2022

Gamescom Hunt

BETA KEYS! Those are what people could get when visiting LabyMod at Gamescom 2022 in Cologne. However, to get into the LabyMod 4 closed beta was not that easy. We had a well received game in our mobile app which featured a scavenger hunt. People had to follow the compass at Gamescom in real life and answer questions. A beta key was available from a staff member after completing the game.

18.11.2022

LabyMod 4 Closed Beta

Finally it was there! We invited the first people to play with our new version of LabyMod. LabyMod 4 having a fresh design, new features and supporting the most recent versions could be experienced by the user for the first time. Together with our testers we are searching for issues and are constantly improving our codebase. We also made it possible to track our progress with our new blog.

2023

20.06.2023

The LabyMod Launcher

We made it possible to start LabyMod in a much more comfortable way. Our very own launcher was released due to changes to Minecraft’s official one. The launcher improves your experience of starting and configuring LabyMod with auto updates, an easy version selection and an easy way to set up your RAM usage.

07.07.2023

LabyMod 4 Release

The time has come! Use your chance to play with the best Minecraft modification we have ever made: LabyMod 4! Our modern design brings the look of the game to the next level. Experience LabyMod’s great performance, use our addons and your very own Fabric mods and enjoy our variety of features to enhance your game experience! Download our new and easy to use launcher now and have fun playing with LabyMod 4!

07.07.2023

A new generation of emotes

Do you want to impress your friends? We’ve added a completely new type of emotes with our new EPIC and LEGENDARY emotes. Our fabulous, well animated and very advanced emotes guarantee everyone looking at you. You can configure your emotes in our freshly reworked dashboard!
